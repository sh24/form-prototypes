SH24 - The sexual health service

SH24 is a free, convenient and discrete sexual health check up. Get a test kit sent you by post. The test takes 10 minutes and you simply post it back in the envelope provided.

Get tested for...

  • ✓Chlamydia
  • ✓Gonorrhoea
  • ✓Syphilis
  • ✓HIV

Key facts...

  • SH24 is free to use if you live in Lambeth or Southwark
  • Your private data will be stored securely on SH24 servers
  • Personal information about you and your results will never be shared with anyone
